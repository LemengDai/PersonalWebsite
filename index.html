Some of My Works & Activities

Flappy Bird Game

I used the Pygame module of Python to create the game for the create task of 2020 AP CSP. It is a remake of a game called Flappy Bird. The player needs to control the bird to move through gaps between pipes, the player loses if the bird collides with the pipes or the ground. The video illustrates that the player can make the bird fly upwards by pressing the up key, otherwise the bird would drop to the ground and therefore the player loses. The bird needs to fly through the pipes, flying through each pair of pipes worth one point. In the end, the player can replay the game by pressing the enter key.

Watch the video on YouTube
View the source code of the project on GitHub

Smart Door Receptionist with Smart Lock System

I created this smart security system with the Raspberry Pi. It is used to see a visitor when the house's door is closed. Whenever a visitor rings the bell, an image of the visitor is captured by the camera and then sent to the email address of the house's owner. The owner then can go to a website where he/she can choose to unlock the door or keep the door locked.

Watch the video on YouTube
View the source code of the project on GitHub

Inspiration

Leonardo da Vinci

When people talk about inspirational engineers, they might mention people like Elon Musk and Steve Jobs. I definitely admire them a lot. However, the engineer who brings me the most inspiration is Leonardo da Vinci, which might sound strange. He is most famous for his drawings, but he finally became my role model because he is a literal combination of science, art and engineering. I want to become someone like him who has interests and works in multidiscipline, someone who has both a precise attitude towards science and unlimited creativity.

How to Walk on Water and Climb up Walls

The book was written by David Hu, which talks about the remarkable mechanics behind animal locomotion, as well as the robots and devices that are inspired by animal mechanics. This is the book that makes me determined for engineering. I want to be part of the creation process of innovative projects and it does not matter if I am the engineer who designs and builds them or the programmer who programs them.

"People expect scientists to save gas as they go from A to B. But the real power of science is to take us to destinations that we have never been to." -- David Hu

"I hope it marks your beginning as you see your world and the animals in it a little differently, with an eye for the details, a heart open to strangeness and wonder, and a commitment to always asking why." -- David Hu
